Collection of cool services made by others, usually free.

- [Send](https://send.vis.ee/) — file sharing
- [Hat.sh](https://hat.sh/) — file encryption
- [Piped](https://piped.video/) — YouTube frontend
- [CryptPad](https://cryptpad.fr/) — Cloud Office Suite
- [PrivateBin](https://0.0g.gg/) — PasteBin alternative
- [FreeDNS](https://freedns.afraid.org/) — free subdomains
- [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) — free static site hosting
- [Telegram Cloud](https://telegram.org/) — unlimited free storage
- [lofi atc](https://www.lofiatc.com/) — Lo-Fi beats with ATC sounds
